# ChemicalGibbsFreeEnergyDifference

**Source:** `models/kwn/ChemicalGibbsFreeEnergyDifference.py`

Chemical Gibbs free energy difference for a set of species.

Computes $\Delta g = \sum_i \Delta x_i \, (\mu_i - \mu_i^{\mathrm{eq}})$
where $\Delta x_i$ is the concentration difference for species
$i$, $\mu_i$ is the chemical potential in the matrix, and
$\mu_i^{\mathrm{eq}}$ is the equilibrium chemical potential.

## Inputs

`concentration_differences` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Concentration differences for each species


## Outputs

`chemical_gibbs_free_energy` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Chemical Gibbs free energy difference


## Parameters

`chemical_potentials` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Chemical potentials in the matrix

`equilibrium_potentials` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Equilibrium chemical potentials
