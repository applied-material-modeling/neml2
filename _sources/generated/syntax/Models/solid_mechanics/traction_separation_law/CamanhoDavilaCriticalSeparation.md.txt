# CamanhoDavilaCriticalSeparation

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/traction_separation_law/CamanhoDavilaCriticalSeparation.py`

Camanho-Davila mixed-mode critical (damage-onset) separation. Opening branch:
$\delta_c = \delta_{n0} \delta_{s0} \sqrt{1+\beta^2} / \sqrt{\delta_{s0}^2 + \beta^2 \delta_{n0}^2}$
with $\delta_{n0} = N/K$ and $\delta_{s0} = S/K$.
Compression branch: $\delta_c = \delta_{s0}$.

## Inputs

`normal_separation` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Normal separation (typically the Macaulay-positive part of the normal jump; used only to determine the opening / compression branch)


## Outputs

`critical_separation` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Critical (damage-onset) separation


## Parameters

`mode_mixity` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Mode-mixity ratio. May be wired to an upstream `ModeMixity` (nonlinear-capable).

`penalty_stiffness` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Penalty stiffness K

`normal_strength` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Tensile (normal) strength N

`shear_strength` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Shear strength S
