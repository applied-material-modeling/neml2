# VoceIsotropicHardening

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/plasticity/VoceIsotropicHardening.py`

Voce isotropic hardening model, $h = R \left[ 1 - \exp(-d \bar{\varepsilon}_p) \right]$,
where $R$ is the isotropic
hardening upon saturation, and $d$ is the hardening rate.

## Inputs

`equivalent_plastic_strain` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Equivalent plastic strain


## Outputs

`isotropic_hardening` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Isotropic hardening


## Parameters

`saturated_hardening` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Saturated isotropic hardening

`saturation_rate` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Hardening saturation rate
