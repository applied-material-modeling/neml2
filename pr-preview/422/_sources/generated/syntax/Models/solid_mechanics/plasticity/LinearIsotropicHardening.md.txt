# LinearIsotropicHardening

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/plasticity/LinearIsotropicHardening.py`

Map equivalent plastic strain to isotropic hardening following a linear
relationship, i.e., $h = K \bar{\varepsilon}_p$ where $K$ is
the hardening modulus.

## Inputs

`equivalent_plastic_strain` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Equivalent plastic strain


## Outputs

`isotropic_hardening` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Isotropic hardening


## Parameters

`hardening_modulus` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Hardening modulus
