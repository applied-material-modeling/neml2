# AdvectiveStress

**Source:** `models/porous_flow/AdvectiveStress.py`

Variational advective stress associated with swelling-induced volume change.

Implements $p_s = -c/(3 J_s^{5/3} J_t^{2/3}) * P_{ij} F_{ij}$ where
$J_s$ (swelling/phase-change Jacobian) and $J_t$ (thermal-expansion
Jacobian) are optional inputs defaulting to ``1``, $P$ is the first
Piola-Kirchhoff stress, $F$ is the deformation gradient, and $c$ is
the volume-change coefficient. Mirrors the C++
``models/porous_flow/AdvectiveStress`` set_value math 1:1.

## Inputs

`js` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar`
: The Jacobian of the deformation gradient associated with the swelling and phase change

`jt` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar`
: The Jacobian of the deformation gradient associated with the thermal and volume expansion

`deformation_gradient` &mdash; `input` &middot; `R2` &middot; **required**
: The deformation gradient

`pk1_stress` &mdash; `input` &middot; `R2` &middot; **required**
: 1st Piola-Kirchhoff stress


## Outputs

`advective_stress` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: The average advective stress


## Parameters

`coefficient` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Coefficient c
