# KineticFactor

**Source:** `models/kwn/KineticFactor.py`

Nucleation kinetic factor.

Computes :math:`\beta = 4\pi \, (N_a / V_m)^{4/3} \, R_{\mathrm{crit}}^2 /
\mathrm{sum}`, where :math:`R_{\mathrm{crit}}` is the critical radius for
nucleation, :math:`\mathrm{sum}` is the projected diffusivity sum,
:math:`V_m` is the molar volume of the precipitate, and :math:`N_a` is
Avogadro's number.

## Inputs

`critical_radius` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Critical radius for nucleation

`projected_diffusivity_sum` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Projected diffusivity sum


## Outputs

`kinetic_factor` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Kinetic factor for nucleation


## Parameters

`molar_volume` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Molar volume of the precipitate

`avogadro_number` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Avogadro's number
