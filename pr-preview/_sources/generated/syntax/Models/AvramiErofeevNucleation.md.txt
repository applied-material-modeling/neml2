# AvramiErofeevNucleation

**Source:** `models/chemical_reactions/AvramiErofeevNucleation.py`

Avrami--Erofeev nucleation reaction mechanism.

Maps the conversion degree :math:`a` to the reaction rate

.. math:: f = k (1 - a) \bigl(-\ln(1 - a)\bigr)^n

where :math:`k` is the reaction coefficient and :math:`n` is the reaction
order.

## Inputs

`conversion_degree` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Degree of conversion


## Outputs

`reaction_rate` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Reaction rate


## Parameters

`coef` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Reaction coefficient

`order` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Reaction order
