# DamagedStress

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/damage/DamagedStress.py`

Apply isotropic scalar damage to a stress tensor:

.. math::
    \boldsymbol{\sigma} = (1 - D)\,\tilde{\boldsymbol{\sigma}}

where :math:`D \in [0, 1)` is the scalar damage variable and
:math:`\tilde{\boldsymbol{\sigma}}` is the undamaged (effective) stress.
Used as the final stage of any continuum-damage-mechanics composition;
paired with `MazarsDamage` or `MazarsDamageStressAlpha`
in Mazars CDM input files.

## Inputs

`damage` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Scalar damage variable D

`effective_stress` &mdash; `input` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: Undamaged effective stress sigma_tilde


## Outputs

`stress` &mdash; `output` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: Nominal (damaged) stress sigma
