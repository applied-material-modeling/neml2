# OrientationRate

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/crystal_plasticity/OrientationRate.py`

Defines the rate of the crystal orientations as a spin given by
$\Omega^e = w - w^p - \varepsilon d^p + d^p \varepsilon$ where
$\Omega^e = \dot{Q} Q^T$, $Q$ is the orientation, $w$ is
the vorticity, $w^p$ is the plastic vorticity, $d^p$ is the
plastic deformation rate, and $\varepsilon$ is the elastic stretch.

## Inputs

`elastic_strain` &mdash; `input` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: The name of the elastic strain tensor

`vorticity` &mdash; `input` &middot; `WR2` &middot; **required**
: The name of the vorticity tensor

`plastic_deformation_rate` &mdash; `input` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: The name of the plastic deformation rate

`plastic_vorticity` &mdash; `input` &middot; `WR2` &middot; **required**
: The name of the plastic vorticity


## Outputs

`orientation_rate` &mdash; `output` &middot; `WR2` &middot; **required**
: The name of the orientation rate
