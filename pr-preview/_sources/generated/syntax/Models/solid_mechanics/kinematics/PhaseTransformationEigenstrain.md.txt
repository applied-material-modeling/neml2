# PhaseTransformationEigenstrain

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/kinematics/PhaseTransformationEigenstrain.py`

Define the (cummulative, as opposed to instantaneous) linear isotropic phase
transformation (from phase A to phase B) eigenstrain, i.e.
$\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}_\mathrm{PT} = \Delta V f \boldsymbol{I}$, where $\Delta V$ is the
volume fraction change when going from phase A to B, $f$
is the phase fraction (0 to 1, A to B).

## Inputs

`phase_fraction` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Phase fraction

`volume_fraction_change` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Change in volume fraction going from phase A to phase B


## Outputs

`eigenstrain` &mdash; `output` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: Eigenstrain
