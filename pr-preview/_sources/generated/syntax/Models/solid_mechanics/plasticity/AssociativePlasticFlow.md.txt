# AssociativePlasticFlow

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/plasticity/AssociativePlasticFlow.py`

Map the flow rate (i.e., the consistency parameter in the KKT conditions)
to the rate of internal variables. This object calculates the rate of
plastic strain following associative flow rule, i.e.
$\dot{\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}}_p = - \dot{\gamma} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \boldsymbol{M}}$,
where
$\dot{\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}}_p$ is the plastic strain,
$\dot{\gamma}$ is the flow rate, $f$ is the yield function, and
$\boldsymbol{M}$ is the Mandel stress.

## Inputs

`flow_rate` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Flow rate

`flow_direction` &mdash; `input` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: Flow direction which can be calculated using Normality


## Outputs

`plastic_strain_rate` &mdash; `output` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: Rate of plastic strain
