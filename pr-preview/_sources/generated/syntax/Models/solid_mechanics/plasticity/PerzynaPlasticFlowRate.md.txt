# PerzynaPlasticFlowRate

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/plasticity/PerzynaPlasticFlowRate.py`

Perzyna's viscous approximation of the consistent yield envelope (with a
power law), i.e. $\dot{\gamma} = \left( \frac{\left< f \right>}{\eta} \right)^n$, where $f$ is
the yield function, $\eta$ is the
reference stress, and $n$ is the power-law exponent.

## Inputs

`yield_function` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Yield function


## Outputs

`flow_rate` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Flow rate


## Parameters

`reference_stress` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Reference stress

`exponent` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Power-law exponent
