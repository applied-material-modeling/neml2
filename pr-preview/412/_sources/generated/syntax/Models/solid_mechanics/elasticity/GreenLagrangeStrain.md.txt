# GreenLagrangeStrain

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/elasticity/GreenLagrangeStrain.py`

Green-Lagrange strain, $E = \frac{1}{2} (C - I)$, where
$C = F^T F$ is the right Cauchy-Green tensor and $I$ is the
identity tensor.

## Inputs

`deformation_gradient` &mdash; `input` &middot; `R2` &middot; **required**
: The deformation gradient


## Outputs

`strain` &mdash; `output` &middot; `SR2` &middot; **required**
: The Green-Lagrange strain
