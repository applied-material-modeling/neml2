# ThermalDeformationJacobian

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/kinematics/ThermalDeformationJacobian.py`

Define the linear isotropic thermal deformation Jacobian, i.e. $J = 1 + \alpha (T - T_0)$,
where $\alpha$ is the coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE), $T$ is
the temperature, and $T_0$ is the reference (stress-free) temperature.

## Inputs

`temperature` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Temperature


## Outputs

`jacobian` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Thermal deformation Jacobian


## Parameters

`reference_temperature` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Reference (stress-free) temperature

`CTE` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Coefficient of thermal expansion
