# KocksMeckingYieldStress

**Source:** `models/solid_mechanics/plasticity/KocksMeckingYieldStress.py`

The yield stress given by the Kocks-Mecking model. $\sigma_y = \exp{C} \mu$ with $\mu$ the
shear modulus and $C$ the
horizontal intercept from the Kocks-Mecking diagram.

## Outputs

`yield_stress` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Output name of the yield stress


## Parameters

`C` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: The Kocks-Mecking horizontal intercept

`shear_modulus` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: The shear modulus
