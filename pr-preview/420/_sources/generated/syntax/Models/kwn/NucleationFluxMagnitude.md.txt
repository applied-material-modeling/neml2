# NucleationFluxMagnitude

**Source:** `models/kwn/NucleationFluxMagnitude.py`

Magnitude of the nucleation flux (excluding the Dirac delta term).

Computes $J = Z \, \beta \, N_0 \, \exp\!\bigl(-\Delta g / (k T)\bigr)$,
where $Z$ is the Zeldovich factor, $\beta$ is the kinetic
factor, $\Delta g$ is the nucleation barrier, $T$ is the
temperature, $N_0$ is the nucleation site density, and $k$ is
the Boltzmann constant.

## Inputs

`zeldovich_factor` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Zeldovich factor

`kinetic_factor` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Kinetic factor

`nucleation_barrier` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Nucleation barrier

`temperature` &mdash; `input` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Temperature


## Outputs

`nucleation_flux_magnitude` &mdash; `output` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Nucleation flux magnitude excluding the Dirac delta term


## Parameters

`nucleation_site_density` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Nucleation site density

`boltzmann_constant` &mdash; `parameter` &middot; `Scalar` &middot; **required**
: Boltzmann constant
