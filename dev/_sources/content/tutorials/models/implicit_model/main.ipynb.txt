    "# Implicit models\n",
    "\n",
    "In this tutorial, we'll build a Perzyna viscoplasticity update whose plastic strain is\n",
    "defined as the root of a nonlinear residual, wrap it in an\n",
    "`ImplicitUpdate` so a Newton solver finds that root, and then\n",
    "differentiate through the solve.\n",
    "\n",
    "Stiff viscoplastic flow is the canonical case: the plastic-strain\n",
    "increment depends on the stress *after* the increment, which in turn\n",
    "depends on the plastic strain — so the update has no closed form.\n",
    "The standard move is to write a residual that vanishes at the right\n",
    "answer and let a solver find it; in this tutorial the residual is\n",
    "assembled from leaves already in NEML2's catalog."

    "## The physics\n",
    "\n",
    "The Perzyna viscoplastic update we will solve is, in continuous form,\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{align}\n",
    "  \\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^e &= \\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon} - \\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p, \\\\\n",
    "  \\boldsymbol{\\sigma} &= 3K\\,\\operatorname{vol}\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^e\n",
    "                       + 2G\\,\\operatorname{dev}\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^e, \\\\\n",
    "  \\bar{\\sigma} &= J_2(\\boldsymbol{\\sigma}), \\\\\n",
    "  f^p &= \\bar{\\sigma} - \\sigma_y, \\\\\n",
    "  \\boldsymbol{N} &= \\partial f^p / \\partial \\boldsymbol{\\sigma}, \\\\\n",
    "  \\dot{\\gamma} &= \\left(\\frac{\\langle f^p \\rangle}{\\eta}\\right)^{n}, \\\\\n",
    "  \\dot{\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}}^p &= \\dot{\\gamma}\\,\\boldsymbol{N}.\n",
    "\\end{align}\n",
    "\n",
    "Backward-Euler time integration converts the rate equation\n",
    "$\\dot{\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}}^p = \\dot{\\gamma} \\boldsymbol{N}$ into\n",
    "the residual\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "  \\mathbf{r}(\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p)\n",
    "  = \\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p\n",
    "    - \\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p_n\n",
    "    - (t - t_n)\\,\\dot{\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}}^p,\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "whose root is the updated plastic strain $\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p$.\n",
    "Everything that feeds $\\dot{\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}}^p$ — elasticity,\n",
    "yield function, normality, the flow rate — depends on the unknown\n",
    "$\\boldsymbol{\\varepsilon}^p$, which is what makes the problem\n",
    "implicit. Solving this residual is the **return-mapping algorithm**\n",
    "familiar from the solid-mechanics literature.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Building the residual\n",

    "and the residual is the `ComposedModel` that wires them together. The\n",
    "full input file:"

    "## Differentiating through the solve\n",
    "\n",
    "The wrapped model is differentiable end-to-end through the Newton\n",
    "solve. Once the residual is zero, the implicit function theorem gives\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "  \\frac{\\partial \\mathbf{s}}{\\partial \\mathbf{g}}\n",
    "  = -\\left(\\frac{\\partial \\mathbf{r}}{\\partial \\mathbf{s}}\\right)^{-1}\n",
    "     \\frac{\\partial \\mathbf{r}}{\\partial \\mathbf{g}},\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "and `ImplicitUpdate` reuses the converged Jacobian factorization to\n",
    "apply it in the backward pass — no unrolling of the Newton iterations.\n",
    "We can drive the prescribed strain with a scalar load multiplier\n",
    "`alpha`, push it through the solver, read off a scalar functional of\n",
    "the plastic-strain answer, and call `.backward()`:"
   ]
  },
  {
   "cell_type": "code",
   "execution_count": null,
   "id": "6081df01",
   "metadata": {},
   "outputs": [],
   "source": [
    "alpha = torch.tensor(1.0, dtype=torch.float64, requires_grad=True)\n",
    "strain_base = torch.tensor([0.01, 0.005, -0.001, 0.0, 0.0, 0.0], dtype=torch.float64)\n",
    "strain_v = SR2(alpha * strain_base)\n",
    "\n",
    "(ps,) = model(strain_v, guess, plastic_strain_n, t, t_n)\n",
    "eqv_plastic_strain = torch.sqrt(torch.tensor(2.0 / 3.0)) * ps.data.norm()\n",
    "eqv_plastic_strain.backward()\n",
    "\n",
    "float(eqv_plastic_strain.detach()), float(alpha.grad)"
